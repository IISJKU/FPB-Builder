Flex Picture eBook

The European Flex Picture Ebook project is the fruit of collaborative work between 7 European organisations specialising in visual, hearing and cognitive impairment, as well as digital accessibility and a publishing specialist. To the website

Institut Integriert Studieren (IIS)

is a student support and research institute focusing on social integration, e-accessibility and inclusive education. It supports over 100 disabled students and conducts research projects at local and international level. It is also the main promoter of the International Conference on Computers Assisting People with Disabilities (ICCHP), which is a world leader in the field of digital inclusion. To the website
