Welcome to the Chameleon collection, the collection that offers you the possibility of adapting your book according to your needs or desires: by allowing you to modify the illustrations, by choosing a particular type of writing, a background colour, by activating or not the highlighting of syllables, or even the audio version... In short, it's up to you to make your choice, it's the book that adapts to you! You can also isolate and/or zoom in on the elements of the illustration for the pleasure of discovery!

The menu

When you have finished making settings, we advise you to exit the book and return to it, all the settings will then be saved and displayed directly. When you make a change while reading, you should also exit and return, otherwise the changes are sometimes only displayed after a few pages.

The text menu

Font

Choose the type of writing you prefer, i.e. the one that will allow you to read more easily and... Click on it!

If you want to enlarge or reduce the font size, you can go to the reader menu.

Background

Choose the colour behind the text that best suits your vision and... Click on it!

Please note that you can only change the background colour with this menu.

If you change the background colour via the player menu, there may be interference.

Highlighting

If you need to have a specific reading aid to better analyse the different sounds and syllables. Then click on "activate".

If you do not need it, click on "deactivate".

Confirm / Reset

Once you have chosen the font, background and highlighting that suits you... Click on "confirm"!

If you prefer the initial proposals, namely :

"Luciole"

"White background"

"Highlighting: disabled"

Click on "reset"!

The image menu

Each number corresponds to a different version of the image. The larger the number, the cleaner the design. Conversely, the smaller the number, the more detailed the design.

It's up to you to choose your version... Click on the number that suits you!

Do you prefer to see the drawings in black and white or in colour?

Click on the small circle to choose either coloured or black and white.

Example of image simplification

Simplification button

Do you want the number corresponding to the chosen version to appear at the bottom of the page?

If so... Click on "Display"! You can then change the version at any time during the story!

If not... Click on "Hide"!

Black and White Button

Do you want the little circle corresponding to the colour or black/white option you have chosen to appear at the bottom of each page?

If so... Click on "Show"! You can then change this option at any time during the story!

If not... Click on "Hide"!

Validate / Reset

When you have made your choices concerning the display of the buttons as well as the version and the colour or black/white option that are most suitable...

Then click on "validate"!

If you prefer the initial proposals, namely:

"Show version button"

"Show black and white button"

"Version 1"

"Colour option" Click on "Reset"!

The audio menu

Audio button

If you want to see the audio button of the text on the screen ... Click on "show"! You will then have to press this button to hear the actor!

You can stop it at any time by pressing the pause button

For a playback without audio... Click on "hide"!

Audio playback speed

If you want the playback to be faster... Click on "plus"!

If you want the playback to be slower... Click on "less"!

During playback

Option to isolate an element

If you prefer to look at one part of the illustration first, you can do so! Just click on the element in question. You will then see that the rest of the illustration will fade away... This will allow you to concentrate on the chosen image!

Zoom option

If you want to see a part

of the illustration larger,

you just have to put yourself on the chosen picture and either stay on it for 2 seconds or double click on it. The image is enlarged!

When you have finished, click on the red square with the white cross in the top right-hand corner!

Animation of illustrations

On some illustrations, you will see the play button at the bottom

If you press it... The picture suddenly comes to life! Indeed, it becomes animated and can also make some little noises!

Sound animation

On some pictures, you will see the speaker button at the bottom of the screen.

When you press it... you can hear funny noises!

Now all we have to do is wish you a good discovery!
